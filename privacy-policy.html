Privacy Policy for Good Morning India

Last updated: April 20, 2026

Introduction

Good Morning India ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application ("App") handles information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

Our App is designed with privacy in mind and collects minimal information:

  • Device Information: We do not collect or store any personal device information.
  • User Preferences: Your notification settings and time preferences are stored locally on your device only.
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information.

Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use information such as:

  • Device advertising ID
  • IP address
  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage data for ad personalization

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

Firebase Storage

We use Firebase Storage to deliver daily greeting images. No personal data is collected or stored through this service.

Permissions

Our App requests the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To display daily morning greetings at your chosen time
  • Exact Alarms: To ensure notifications are delivered precisely at your scheduled time
  • Boot Complete: To reschedule notifications after device restart
  • Internet: To load greeting images and display advertisements

Data Storage

All your preferences (notification time, language selection) are stored locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your information to third parties. The only data sharing occurs through:

  • Share Feature: When you explicitly choose to share greetings via WhatsApp or Facebook, only the image you select is shared through those platforms' APIs.
  • AdMob: As described above, for advertisement delivery.

Children's Privacy

Our App is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children or any users.

Data Retention

Since we store data only locally on your device, you have complete control. Uninstalling the App will remove all stored preferences.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Control ad personalization through your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads)
  • Reset your advertising ID at any time
  • Delete all App data by uninstalling the App

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Security

Since we do not collect or transmit personal data, your privacy is inherently protected. All preferences remain on your local device with standard Android security protections.
